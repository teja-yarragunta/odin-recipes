Delicious Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originates from the Indian subcontinent. It is made by layering spiced rice with marinated meat or vegetables and slow-cooking them to perfection.

This recipe will help you prepare a delightful and satisfying biryani, perfect for special occasions or a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro and mint leaves
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 teaspoons biryani masala powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices: 4 cloves, 2 cardamom pods, 1 cinnamon stick, 2 bay leaves
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pot. Fry the onions until golden brown and set aside half for garnishing.
  3. In the same pot, add whole spices and saut√© until aromatic.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and chicken pieces. Cook until the chicken is lightly browned.
  5. Mix in the chopped tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and biryani masala. Cook until the tomatoes break down.
  6. Add yogurt, cilantro, and mint leaves. Cook for another 5 minutes until well combined.
  7. Layer the rice over the chicken mixture. Pour 4 cups of water and sprinkle some fried onions on top.
  8. Drizzle saffron milk (optional) over the rice.
  9. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes until the rice is fluffy and the chicken is cooked.
  10. Garnish with the reserved fried onions.
  11. Serve hot with raita or a side of your choice. Enjoy!
