Delicious Ramen Recipe

Description

Ramen is a traditional Japanese noodle soup packed with rich flavors. This recipe features savory broth, tender noodles, and a variety of delicious toppings such as soft-boiled eggs, green onions, and marinated pork slices.

Perfect for a comforting and hearty meal, this ramen recipe will bring authentic Japanese cuisine straight to your home kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • 1 cup sliced green onions
  • 200g marinated pork slices (chashu)
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed), cut into strips
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken broth, water, soy sauce, and miso paste. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer.
  2. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Add sesame oil to the broth for enhanced flavor.
  4. To serve, divide the cooked noodles into bowls.
  5. Ladle the hot broth over the noodles.
  6. Top each bowl with soft-boiled eggs, sliced green onions, marinated pork, and strips of nori.
  7. Enjoy your homemade delicious ramen!
